About Us

TANO FABRICATORS LTD is a specialist fabrication hub where precision meets power. With expertise in MIG, TIG, and Stick welding, we transform raw metal into durable structures for the construction and manufacturing industries. Every spark in our shop represents a commitment to discipline and structural integrity. From intricate stainless steel components to heavy-duty repairs, our team ensures every joint is seamless, strong, and built to stand the test of time."
